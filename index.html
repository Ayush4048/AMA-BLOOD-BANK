About Us

About Blood Doners

AMA Blood Bank Society is a noble charity organization formed with the active initiation and guidance of Cheif Minster Shri Yogi AdityaNath under Act For Humanity. Act For Humanity is a Non-profit organization founded by Mr.Anand Kushwaha , Mr. Mayank Srivastav and Mr. Ayush Keshrwani and a group of young social workers during 2021. Act For Humanity aims to enhance the humanistic elements of each individual and prepare them to be tolerant, responsive, accountable and responsible social citizen.

AMA Blood Bank is aimed at promoting the awareness of blood donation among the public. It is committed to stay ahead of all linguistic-rational-religious-political differences and shall be fully focusing its objectives in health care activities, with technology support from WebCastle Media Pvt. Ltd., Cochin.

AMA Blood Bank Society is registered as per The TCLSCS Registration Act XII of 1955 under the Government of Uttar pradesh. Initial operation of AMA Blood Bank shall be limited to Uttar Pradesh and in the coming years its services shall be available all over India.
